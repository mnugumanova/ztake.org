Description of Services

As validator we operate a full-node on the Cosmos network. We participate in consensus by broadcasting votes which contain cryptographic signatures signed with our private key.

Validator ApS operate a highly available validator infrastructure for the Cosmos Network featuring:

  • Proactive 24/7 monitoring and on-call personnel
  • Secret key material secured by Hardware Security Modules
  • Fully redundant hardware and co-location environment
  • Fully redundant network connectivity with dedicated bandwidth
  • High level of operational automation for scaling and predictability
  • 20+ years of IT operations experience

To completely align our interests with those of our delegators, Validator ApS will be staking through our own service.

Validator ApS’ infrastructure is designed from the ground up for security, high availability and to eliminate any single point of failure.

Validator ApS use a hybrid of cloud and physical servers. Cloud is used for scalability and high availability, while the physical servers are used to secure sensitive key material.
