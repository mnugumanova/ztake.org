About

We are early blockchain adopters with an extensive background in tech space. We have both worked for various size IT companies in the past including Amazon and Salesforce. Our tech lead - Roman is a top-notch blockchain engineer with a particular passion to smart contracts and Mira (COO) is a certified Washington notary who has been a long time PoA validator and specializes in Proof of Stake consensus research. Our headquarter is in Seattle, US, one of the most stable countries on a political, economic, and social level.

We have been actively involved in running and testing the COSMOS testnets as well as participated in Game of Stakes. Due to our deep tech background and connections, we would like to leverage our network to help build value-add products for the Cosmos network.

Description of Services

As a validator we operate a fullnode on the Cosmos network. We participate in consensus by broadcasting votes which contain cryptographic signatures signed with our private key.

Ztake.org operate a highly available validator infrastructure for the Cosmos Network featuring:

  • Proactive 24/7 monitoring and on-call personnel
  • Fully redundant hardware and co-location environment
  • Fully redundant network connectivity with dedicated bandwidth
  • High level of operational automation for scaling and predictability

To completely align our interests with those of our delegators, Ztake.org will be staking through our own service.

Ztake.org’ infrastructure is designed from the ground up for security, high availability and to eliminate any single point of failure.

Ztake.org use a hybrid of cloud and physical servers. Cloud is used for scalability and high availability, while the physical servers are used to secure sensitive key material.
